Frequently asked questions

What is this contest about?

The Bajaj Finserv contest encourages authorised dealers to decorate their stores in a summer theme. The store must be decorated using Bajaj Finserv’s collaterals and follow the summer theme. Three winners will get a chance to win an exclusive cocktail hour with Suresh Raina and Rakul Preet in Mumbai.

Who can participate in the contest?

All authorised dealers who download and print the collaterals for the summer decoration theme and decorate their stores can participate in the contest.

How can I participate in the contest?

Step 1: Decorate your store with the summer-themed collaterals between 10th April to 31st May.

Step 2: Share photos of your decorated store on Instagram with the hashtag #CashKyunEMIKaro and tag @bajajfinserv_emihaina on Instagram for your entry to be considered. The entry and photos have to be submitted between 15th April to 31st May.

Step 3: Expect a surprise visit from our partner.

What is the timeline of the contest?

1. Store decoration period: 10th April to 31st May
2. Photo submission window: 15th April to 31st May
3. Winner announcement: 7th June 2025
4. Exclusive cocktail hour with Suresh Raina & Rakul Preet: 28th June 2025

What are the decoration guidelines?

Use Bajaj Finserv’s collateral like danglers, posters, flyers/leaflets, and videos. You can add additional decorations but ensure they align with the summer theme. You can download the necessary collateral from shared link.

What kind of collaterals are provided?

The decoration collaterals include: danglers, posters, flyers and videos.

Where should I share the decorated store photos?

Share your decorated store photos and videos on your Instagram account using the hashtag #CashKyunEMIKaro. Make sure you tag @bajajfinserv_emihaina on this Instagram post to enter the contest.

How will the winners be selected?

Winners will be selected based on:
1. Creativity and adherence to the summer theme.
2. Effective use of Bajaj Finserv’s collaterals.
3. Overall visual appeal and innovative decorations.
4. Overall sales during this period through Bajaj Finserv.
Final winners will be revealed on 7th June 2025

What are the rewards for winning?

Winning dealers will receive:
1. An all-expenses-paid trip to Mumbai.
2. Exclusive cocktail hour with Suresh Raina and Rakul Preet.
3. Title of the “Best Decorated Store”.

When and where will the winners meet Suresh Raina and Rakul Preet?

The exclusive cocktail hour will take place in Mumbai on 28th June 2025. Final details will be shared with the winners closer to the date.

Can I use additional decorations apart from the provided collaterals?

Yes, additional decorations are allowed, but they should be in adherence to the summer theme.

What should I do if I encounter issues with downloading the collateral?

In case of any issues with downloading or accessing the collateral, reach out to your Area Sales Manager for assistance.

Is there a limit to how many photos I can submit?

There is no limit to the number of photos, but ensure that the photos clearly highlight the summer-themed decorations and use of Bajaj Finserv’s collaterals.

Will there be any surprise visits for judging?

Yes, surprise visits may be conducted to evaluate store decorations before finalising the winners.

How can I track the status of my participation?

Participants will be notified via email or SMS or WhatsApp about the status of their entry, and updates will be shared during the evaluation phase.

What happens if I win but cannot attend the cocktail hour?

If a winner is unable to attend the cocktail hour, alternative arrangements will be communicated, but the cocktail hour experience cannot be transferred.

Who can I contact for further assistance?

For any queries or assistance, please contact your Area Sales Manager.
